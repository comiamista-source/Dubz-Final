WHAT'S NEW (this updated package)
=================================

1. SKIP INTRO/OUTRO MUSIC  (--skip)
   On a manual GitHub run there is now a "Skip ranges" box.
   Type ranges to KEEP ORIGINAL audio (not dub), e.g.:
       0-30,end-45      -> keep first 30s + last 45s as original
       end-60           -> keep last 60s (outro song)
       90-120           -> keep 90s..120s
   "end" = end of video; "end-45" = the last 45 seconds.
   Everything outside the skip ranges is dubbed normally.
   CLI:  python dub_video.py video.mp4 --skip 0-30,end-45
   Note: works per-chunk. With 60s chunks a 0-30 skip keeps the
   whole first 60s. For tighter intros use a smaller --chunk.

2. PER-CHUNK RETRY LOGGING
   The run log now prints exactly which chunk is retrying:
       chunk 47 retry 2/5: <reason>
       chunk 47 FAILED after 5 tries: <reason>

3. MATRIX CAPPED AT 10
   A run processes at most 10 videos (max-parallel: 10).
   Drop more than 10 in the inbox -> first 10 run, rest wait
   for your next manual run.
